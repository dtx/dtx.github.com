Hi, my name is Darshan Sanghani.

When I was a kid, I used to think that there was someone on the other side of the computer screen, following my actions and changing the monitor accordingly. I wished to meet that person some day. Guess what? I did, and everyday I learn more about him. He fascinates me, drives me. If I am the brains then he is the muscle; till now it's been a dream come true relationship and I would love to keep this relation going for as long as I can.

I am not certain of the exact time I got into technology and programming, it just grew onto me. Since then I have made it a point to learn new technologies and apply them to make life simpler.We have been able to achieve so much in the past few years, why stop now? This isn't even the tip of the tip of the iceberg.

I am also a stoic believer of simplicity within technology. Current software industry is nothing but a bunch of blocks built upon the older ones. A smoother progress is only possible if the current interfaces are made as intuitive and extendible as possible. Hide the intricacies behind a facade, make it easy for the users to come up with new ideas for the software; that's what will prevent a software from dying.

Besides working with technology- I play the piano, I listen to classical and alternative rock, I swim, and seldom play basketball.

  • Kinect Media Controller
    A gesture recognizer for Kinect to be integrated with media players to allow controls like play/pause/stop/enlarge and others through hand gestures.
  • SongFinder
    A lyrics search engine using BM25 retrieval formula with Rocchio feedback, also implemented dot clustering, query likelihood methods for experience, user also gets a link to the specific song on YouTube, this was achieved with YouTube API. This was done on LemurfCGI search engine template.
  • DebtManager
    An iOS application on to help students, friends and people with informal/casual debts.
  • VoIP for Musicians
    A VoIP application from scratch in Python, using a SIP library (PJSip). The idea is to help musicians jam online; we plan on minimizing the latency issues by compressing the audio before dispatch.
